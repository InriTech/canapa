Benefits for Pets

  • May help reduce anxiety and stress
  • Can support joint health and mobility
  • May help with skin conditions
  • Can promote calm during storms/fireworks
  • May support overall wellness
Note: Always consult with your veterinarian before starting your pet on CBD, especially if they have any medical conditions or are on medication.

Dosage Guide

Small Pets (under 10kg): 0.25ml (2.5mg CBD) twice daily

Medium Pets (10-25kg): 0.5ml (5mg CBD) twice daily

Large Pets (25kg+): 1ml (10mg CBD) twice daily

Method: Add to food or administer directly in mouth

Tip: Start with half the recommended dose for the first 3-5 days to allow your pet to adjust.
